Wedding Flowers

Adorn your wedding ceremony with stunning wedding flowers with help from us! Additionally, our custom holiday arrangements help create a cheery atmosphere for any occasions. Our goal is to ensure that you receive the flowers you need and want for your amazing wedding day.

Here Comes the Bride

With a huge selection of flowers for all customers, we form distinctive designs to meet any bridal needs you may have. We put together specialty floral bouquets for every holiday, holiday season, or event. Our florist shop uses exotic flowers instead of carnations for your convenience.


Funeral Bouquets

When you want to show your support for someone experiencing the loss of a loved one, visit our flower shop. We are able to help you provide funeral flowers to let family and friends know you are thinking of them during their time of mourning. We will customize and design funeral arrangements, wreaths, basket bouquets, casket covers and sympathy potted plants.

Gesture of Sympathy

We create floral arrangements for all of your funeral needs using specialty flowers. As an added convenience, the Waters Edge Flowers offers daily delivery to all local funeral homes to make sure your recipient receives the flower arrangement on time.
