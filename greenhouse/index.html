The strongest greenhouses in the U.S.A.

All Greenhouses can be shipped anywhere in the lower 48 states. Shipping runs 1.20 cents per square foot.

Delivery may be 8-12 weeks long to get it to you. Greenhouse models include steel walls on all 4 sides and a pre-fabricated steel framed door.

All greenhouses are shipped with 6' high sidewalls standard. The center height will be 2-3 feet higher depending on the width of building you order.

If you are ordering within the state, we can deliver and set up the greenhouse for you.

Setup usually takes a weekend for most people. The instructions are clear what makes much faster setup time for our customers.

No heavy lifting required. Only a few basic hand tools like a hammer and gloves are necessary to complete installation.
