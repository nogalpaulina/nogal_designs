Our Restaurant

The Vis Schubben Restaurant (Fish Scales) is owned by brothers Jonas and Marcus, and their father Don. All of them share a passion for cooking. Each brother is deeply inspired by nordic cuisine and they are happy to serve amazing food to their customers as chefs.
Jonas, Marcus and Don opened this family business in 2006. Ever since, customers from all over the world enjoy the atmosphere that they can only find in Vis Schubben.
The interior is absolutely stunning, exuding a raw aesthetic of recycled wood, rustic and desaturated colors and beautiful lighting. This motif of raw and rustic continues into the menu with the dishes centering on creative and contemporary Nordic cuisine. Their menu includes dishes such as rare ribeye with pickled trumpet and fried beech mushrooms, wild watercress and warm karl johan mayo, sea buckthorn, juniper cream, roasted hazelnuts and browned butter.
